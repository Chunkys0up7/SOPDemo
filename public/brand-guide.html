Pursuit Bank — Brand Style Guide

Welcome to the Pursuit Bank Brand Style Guide.

This guide establishes the visual identity standards for all Pursuit Bank materials, including the SOP System interface. Consistent application of these guidelines ensures a professional, trustworthy brand experience across all touchpoints.

Key Principle: The Pursuit brand embodies forward momentum, financial growth, and confident progress toward goals.

Design Specifications

Mark Concept

A clean, modern forward-pointing arrow combining a vertical stem with an ascending geometric form. Symbolizes forward momentum, financial growth, and confident progress in pursuit of goals.

Typography

Primary: Segoe UI / System Sans-serif
Weight: 600 (Semibold) for headings
Weight: 400 (Regular) for body text
Style: Clean, modern, professional

Usage Guidelines

Works as standalone mark or with wordmark. Scales cleanly from favicon size (16×16) to large applications. Maintain clear space around the mark equal to the stem width.

Symbolism

The ascending arrow represents clients moving forward toward their financial goals. The geometric clarity and forward-facing direction convey confidence, innovation, and reliability.

Differentiation

Distinct from traditional banking brands with a directional forward-motion design. Simpler, more contemporary approach that feels dynamic while maintaining banking professionalism.

Digital Applications

Optimized for web and mobile interfaces. SVG format ensures crisp rendering at all sizes. Primary blue (#0052CC) provides excellent contrast and accessibility compliance (WCAG AA).
