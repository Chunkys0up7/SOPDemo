Our SOP system uses a modular atomic design approach for maximum searchability, reusability, and adaptability:

  • Molecules - Multi-step procedures combining atoms (e.g., "Account Setup", "Approval Workflow")
  • Organisms - Complete workflows combining molecules (e.g., "Employee Onboarding", "Wire Transfer Process")
  • SOPs - Full documented procedures with metadata, compliance, quality controls, and troubleshooting

All components include: Rich metadata for search, compliance frameworks, decision logic, quality checkpoints, troubleshooting guidance, and version control.
