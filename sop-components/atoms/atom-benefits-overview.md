# Employee Benefits Overview

Welcome to our comprehensive benefits program! Here's what's available to you.

## Health & Wellness

### Medical Insurance
- **Coverage**: Individual and family plans available
- **Provider**: Blue Cross Blue Shield
- **Premium**: Company pays 80%, employee pays 20%
- **Enrollment**: Within 30 days of hire

### Dental Insurance
- **Coverage**: Preventive, basic, and major services
- **Provider**: Delta Dental
- **Premium**: Company pays 100%

### Vision Insurance
- **Coverage**: Annual eye exams and corrective lenses
- **Provider**: VSP
- **Premium**: Company pays 100%

### Wellness Program
- Free gym membership at partner facilities
- Annual wellness stipend: $500
- On-site yoga classes (Tuesdays & Thursdays)
- Mental health support through EAP

## Financial Benefits

### 401(k) Retirement Plan
- Company matches 50% up to 6% of salary
- Immediate vesting
- Multiple investment options

### Life Insurance
- Company-provided: 2x annual salary
- Optional additional coverage available

### Disability Insurance
- Short-term: 60% of salary for up to 6 months
- Long-term: 60% of salary after 6 months

## Time Off

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
- **Years 0-2**: 15 days per year
- **Years 3-5**: 20 days per year
- **Years 6+**: 25 days per year

### Holidays
- 10 paid company holidays
- 2 floating holidays (your choice)

### Sick Leave
- Unlimited sick days (use responsibly)
- Doctor's note required after 3 consecutive days

### Parental Leave
- **Primary caregiver**: 16 weeks paid
- **Secondary caregiver**: 8 weeks paid

## Professional Development

- **Training Budget**: $2,000 per year
- **Conference Attendance**: 1 conference annually
- **Tuition Reimbursement**: Up to $5,000 per year
- **Certification Support**: Exam fees covered

## Perks

- **Remote Work**: Hybrid schedule (2 days office, 3 days remote)
- **Equipment**: Laptop, monitor, accessories provided
- **Commuter Benefits**: Pre-tax transit/parking
- **Free Lunch**: Catered lunch Wednesdays
- **Snacks & Coffee**: Always available
- **Team Events**: Monthly team building activities

## Enrollment

Contact HR at benefits@company.com or visit the benefits portal at benefits.company.com
