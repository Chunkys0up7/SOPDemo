# Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement

## Agreement Overview

All employees must read and acknowledge the Confidentiality Agreement before accessing company systems.

## Key Terms

### Confidential Information Includes:
- Customer data and contact information
- Proprietary software and source code
- Business strategies and financial data
- Trade secrets and technical documentation
- Employee personal information
- Unpublished product roadmaps

### Employee Obligations:
- Do not share confidential information outside the company
- Use confidential information only for work purposes
- Report suspected breaches immediately
- Return all confidential materials upon termination

## Prohibited Actions

❌ **Never**:
- Share credentials or access tokens
- Discuss confidential matters in public spaces
- Copy company data to personal devices
- Share customer information without authorization
- Post company information on social media

## Reporting Breaches

If you suspect a confidentiality breach:
1. Immediately notify your manager
2. Contact Legal at legal@company.com
3. Document the incident details
4. Preserve any evidence
