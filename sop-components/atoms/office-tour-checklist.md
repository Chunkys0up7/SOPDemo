# Office Tour Checklist

Complete this checklist during your office orientation tour.

## Building Access & Security

- [ ] Received building access badge
- [ ] Tested badge at main entrance
- [ ] Tested badge at parking garage
- [ ] Shown emergency exits on your floor
- [ ] Located fire extinguishers
- [ ] Reviewed evacuation procedures
- [ ] Provided after-hours access phone number

## Workspace & Amenities

- [ ] Located your assigned workspace
- [ ] Found your team's area
- [ ] Shown how to adjust desk/chair
- [ ] Located supply closet
- [ ] Found printer/copier stations
- [ ] Shown conference room locations
- [ ] Learned conference room booking system

## Common Areas

- [ ] Located break rooms/kitchens
- [ ] Found coffee/beverage stations
- [ ] Shown cafeteria/lunch area
- [ ] Located vending machines
- [ ] Found restrooms
- [ ] Shown quiet/focus rooms
- [ ] Located phone booths for calls

## Facilities & Services

- [ ] Shown mail/package room
- [ ] Located IT help desk
- [ ] Found first aid kits
- [ ] Shown bike storage (if applicable)
- [ ] Located shower facilities (if applicable)
- [ ] Found mother's room (if applicable)

## Outdoor & Recreation

- [ ] Shown outdoor seating areas
- [ ] Located walking paths
- [ ] Shown parking lot layout
- [ ] Found visitor parking
- [ ] Located smoking areas (if applicable)

## Important Contacts

- **Building Security**: ext. 9999
- **Facilities**: ext. 5555
- **IT Help Desk**: ext. 4357 (HELP)
- **Emergency**: 911 (then notify security)
