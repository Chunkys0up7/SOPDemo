# Test SOP - Customer Onboarding Process

## Purpose

This procedure establishes a standardized process for onboarding new mortgage customers, ensuring compliance with KYC requirements and providing excellent customer experience from initial contact through account activation.

## Scope

### What IS Covered

- Initial customer contact and application
- Identity verification and KYC compliance
- Account setup and documentation
- Welcome communications and training

### What is NOT Covered

- Existing customer re-onboarding (see SOP-XXX)
- Business account onboarding (see SOP-YYY)

## Audience

This procedure is intended for:
- Manager
- Team Lead

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Symptoms | Root Cause | Solution | Escalation |
|-------|----------|------------|----------|------------|
| Missing documents | Customer cannot upload files | Browser compatibility issue | Ask customer to try different browser or email documents | After 3 attempts, escalate to IT |
| Identity verification fails | System rejects ID | Document quality poor | Request high-resolution scan or photo in good lighting | If persists, schedule video call for manual verification |
